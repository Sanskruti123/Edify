Following is a short descriptive summary of all the above stages/ periods that startup may undergo.

1. Survival Period: The name given to this stage completely describes what a startup should act. This period being the initialisation of startup to undergo the challenging situation, the most significant step is to continue the sole existence of startup, with enough financial resources, and without any pending costs, debts, etc. The time for this period may vary between 2-3 months.



Thus, if any startup survives this period of the situation, there are much more chances of it to overcome the challenge it is facing.

2. Switching Period: After the initial step, the startup may assess some threats that situation has posed for its business. For this, the startup must analyse these threats properly and eliminate them with present/ new solutions. And it is this period wherein the startup will decide the exact journey through this situation. This period will vary between 2-3 months but is a very crucial stage from decision making point of view.



The pivoting of the startup is the most important step of this period. Startup must consider various factors affecting it at the present, do proper value-stream analysis and then pitch the idea relating to it.


3. Execution & Marketing Period: This period will continue for almost 1-2 months and will mark the termination of the challenging situation. At this stage, the work load over startup will increase, and the risk will increase as well. So, proper execution of ideas must be done as planned by startups.



4. Growth Period: This is just a small period wherein the startup may able to view the outcomes of its efforts. As per the outcomes, one may assess the business as a success or failure. Even if there is not any appreciable profit obtained over this situation, the startup should be applauded for its work. The journey thereafter depends upon the market, competitors, pivoting strategy implemented and many such factors.


So, to summarise this whole process in few lines, the process must initiate as soon as the challenging situation arises. All the actions must be taken under proper guidance of experienced mentors.
