Introduction-


White Hat Jr. is a Mumbai based EdTech startup by Mr. Karan Bajaj that focuses on training and getting young kids acquainted to computer coding. It is an online platform made for children (ages 6 to 18) to learn programming and are then encouraged to create games, animations and applications. The startup works with a live 1:1 online teaching format and a thoroughly researched curriculum. The subjects range from data structure, app and game development to machine learning and space technology.
